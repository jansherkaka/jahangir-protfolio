About Jahangir

A billing partner who treats your revenue like it's his own

I'm a Revenue Cycle Management specialist who runs end-to-end medical billing for healthcare providers, clinics, and growing practices. From the first patient registration to the final dollar collected, I make sure nothing slips through the cracks.

My work spans Behavioral Health, DME, Chiropractic, Pediatrics, and many more specialties — each with its own coding nuances and payer rules. That specialty depth is how I keep first-pass acceptance high and aging accounts low, so your team can stay focused on patients instead of paperwork.
